About what we do

Worksheet

Word processing for Jain

Create and edit text documents directly in your browser – no dedicated software required. Several people can work at the same time, and every change is saved automatically.

Import your documents to make them instantly editable, including Microsoft® Word and PDF files. Export your work in .docx, .pdf, .odt, .rtf, .txt or .html format.

Calendar

Integrated online calendars

Spend less time planning and more time doing with sharable calendars that integrate seamlessly with contacts and so you always know what's next. Set your remainders, alarms etc easily.

Schedule events quickly by checking colleagues' availability or layering their calendars in a single view.

Notes

Capture what's important and get more done.

With notes you can capture a quick thought, create bucket lists, sketch ideas and more. And with our cloud you can keep your notes updated across all your devices.

Access, create and edit notes wherever you go – from your computer, phone or tablet – even when there's no connection. Every edit that you make is automatically saved and updated across all devices.
